About Beauty House

Passionate about providing superior beauty & medical services that could meet modern life, Beauty House first opened its doors in 2011.

Beauty House is the epitome of quality and convenience. We understand that time is precious, so we make having beauty & medical treatments easy, offering the possibility to have treatments early mornings and late evenings, seven days a week, with no-appointment-needed services and access to online bookings.

We believe that beauty goes beyond the surface. We believe that feeling good about ourselves improves our confidence and our well-being. We only use the highest quality products, brands and equipment available in the industry, and our professionals are passionate and highly qualified.

All medical services are directly administered or supervised by our medical team. Our medical team will create a treatment plan for all clients and they will monitor and supervise the treatment throughout the whole client journey to ensure exceptional results.

This is a contemporary salon with stylish modern decor. You'll be welcomed with free hot drinks and a classic R&B soundtrack.

Simply call us to arrange a consultation or just mail us.
